# UniMarket UI Identity Starter

This package is the design and experience foundation for the University of Waterloo MVP of **UniMarket**.

The goal is not to make a generic marketplace with black-and-gold accents. The goal is to create a product that feels as though it could only have been built for Waterloo students: premium, local, trusted, quietly immersive, and deeply aware of campus life.

## Core promise

> **A marketplace built for Waterloo students, not strangers.**

## Product feeling

UniMarket should feel like:

- Waterloo campus identity
- premium modern commerce
- verified student trust
- calm, fast interactions
- high-end editorial art direction
- product usability above visual spectacle

It should not feel like:

- a hackathon demo
- a Tailwind template
- a generic marketplace clone
- a university portal
- an AI-generated landing page
- a sports fan page
- a page covered in yellow

## The “could only be Waterloo” test

Before approving a screen, ask:

> If the UniMarket wordmark and yellow accents disappeared, would a Waterloo student still recognize that this experience was made for them?

If the answer is no, add context—not decoration.

Good personalization comes from:

- `@uwaterloo.ca` verification
- co-op and term cycles
- move-in and move-out moments
- campus pickup areas
- Waterloo language
- time-aware greetings
- program and year context
- local marketplace patterns
- original campus-inspired art direction

## Important brand boundary

UniMarket should feel Waterloo-native without falsely presenting itself as an official University of Waterloo product.

Use an independent UniMarket identity. Confirm permission before using official University or Warriors marks in production. Do not merge those marks into the UniMarket logo or imply endorsement.

Suggested footer disclosure:

> UniMarket is an independent student-built platform and is not officially affiliated with the University of Waterloo.

## Non-negotiables

- Mobile-first
- Accessible
- Fast
- Secure
- Data-driven university theming
- No fake activity or fake social proof
- No excessive glassmorphism
- No random AI-style gradients
- No generic startup copy
- No animation that delays an action
